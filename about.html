ABOUT US


Ikoyi Fairview is an upscale hotel located on a one acre enclave within Dolphin Estate; a residential estate in Ikoyi which is without doubt the best and most sought after residential neighborhood in Lagos. We provide 24-hour round the clock security and we are located at about 200meters from the Dolphin Estate Police Post and Dodan Military Barracks

Ikoyi Fairview Apartments has a variety of rooms and suites which are by far the lowest priced within Ikoyi & Victoria Island. Our guarantee is this – if you find another hotel in Ikoyi or Victoria Island priced lower than our quoted rates we will not only match such rates but offer a 5% discount on such rates.

All rooms and suites are exquisitely furnished and equipped with a Kitchen/kitchenette (blenders, crockery, fridge & microwave) except the Standard rooms which is equipped with a fridge & microwave. Of course the air conditioning is really chilly and designed to pamper our guests. We boldly declare that no picture can ever capture the sheer exquisiteness of the rooms and suites.

Ikoyi Fairview Apartments does not pretend to provide food free (really any ‘free’ food has been added onto the rates). However we provide food to suit each guest budget from as low as ₦700 for a simple breakfast of just egg, bread & beverage.

Ikoyi Fairview Apartments is a 5mins drive from Osborne Foreshore Estate and the prestigious Southern Sun Hotel along Kingsway Road. It is 7mins drive to Central Business District, Marina and 12mins drive to Victoria Island & Lekki Phase 1 Estate
